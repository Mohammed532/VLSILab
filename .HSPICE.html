HSPICE O-2018.09 Major Enhancements in the September 2018 Release

·         Fundamentals

§  1.5x core engine speedup, including DC convergence speedup, for large post-layout designs in advanced nodes

§  1.5-3x performance speedup for FinFET and FDSOI models (BSIM-CMG and BSIM-IMG) with self-heating effects

§  Improved BSIM-BULK model performance in transient and TRANNOISE analyses

§  1.5-2x SNNOISE, HBNOISE, and PHASENOISE performance speedup

§  FSDB waveform format upgraded to support version 5.7 (default is still 5.6)

·         RF

§  Enable and disable noise by blocks and types for HSPICE noise and all RF noise (SNNOISE, PTDNOISE, and PHASENOISE)

§  Support arbitrary frequency-dependent noise for TRANNOISE

§  Support multiple output nodes for SN- and HB-based noise analyses

·         Monte Carlo

§  Sigma Amplification MC output post processing enhancement (for example, Q-Q plotting)

·         New Models

§ BSIMSOI 4.6/4.6.1

§ BSIM-IMG 102.9/102.9.1

§ PSP 103.6

§ HiSIM 3.0.0

§ HiSIM_HV 2.4.0
